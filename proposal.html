Proposal

Goods is a database that stores and categorizes companies by ethical and unethical business practices.

Mission

Most people are not proponents of modern slavery, child labor, or bribery, yet many companies like Apple, Toyota, and Chevron participate in these unethical practices to create the products we buy. The Goods website and Chrome Extension will help consumers spend their money more conscientiously.

Features

The Goods website features "good" and "bad" companies and their alternatives. The website additionally offers a Google Chrome Extension to encourage conscientious consumption when online shopping elsewhere. (Required features: user accounts, administrative back-end, and user-entered content and image uploads.)

Data to Import

For good companies, store data on:

  • Fair margins
  • Fair worker compensation
  • Sustainable waste and manufacturing practices
  • Suitable working conditions
  • Worker satisfaction

For bad companies, store data on:

  • Working condition
  • Slavery
  • Waste
  • Underage employment
  • Poor wages
  • Worked suicides
